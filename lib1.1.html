About Us

Our Story

CKP Library was founded in 2021 by a group of friends who shared a common passion for reading. What started as a small book club has now grown into an online community of enthusiastic readers from all over the world. We are dedicated to promoting the love of reading and providing a platform for readers to connect and share their ideas.
